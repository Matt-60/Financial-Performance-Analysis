# 📊 Financial Performance Analysis – Management Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an **end‑to‑end financial performance analysis** built in **Power BI**, focused on **KPI monitoring, cost structure analysis, and management‑level insights**.

The project simulates a real‑world financial analytics scenario, where financial data is analyzed across **YTD, quarterly, and monthly levels**, and compared against **Previous Year (PY)** and **Budget**.  
The main objective was to transform raw financial results into **clear, decision‑supporting insights for management**.

## 📂 Data
The analysis is based on financial performance data including:
- Revenue
- COGS
- Variable Costs
- OPEX
- Margin and profitability KPIs

The data allows analysis across:
- **YTD**
- **Quarterly (Q1, Q2)**
- **Monthly (e.g. April, February)**
- Selected markets (e.g. **DE**)
- Comparisons vs **PY** and **Budget**

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Monitor key **financial KPIs** (Gross Margin, Contribution Margin, EBITDA)
- Analyze **cost‑to‑revenue ratios**
- Identify **drivers of performance deviations** vs PY and Budget
- Understand **quarterly and monthly trends**
- Deliver **management‑ready conclusions**

---

## 🛠 Methodology
1. **KPI definition**
   - Defined margin, profitability, and efficiency KPIs aligned with financial reporting logic
2. **Data preparation & structure**
   - Structured data to enable YTD, quarterly, and monthly comparisons
3. **Power BI reporting**
   - Built interactive dashboards with KPI summaries and detailed breakdowns
4. **Comparative analysis**
   - PY vs Budget vs Actual performance comparison
5. **Insight generation**
   - Translated numerical results into business conclusions

---

## 📊 Key Analytical Areas

### **KPI Analysis**
- Gross Profit Margin  
- Contribution Margin  
- EBITDA  
- Cost‑to‑Revenue ratios (COGS, Variable Costs, OPEX)

### **Cost Structure Analysis**
- Variable Costs (Warehouse, Distribution)
- OPEX with focus on Marketing (Google vs Meta)
- Identification of cost rigidity vs revenue decline

### **Trend Analysis**
- Quarterly performance comparison
- Monthly performance deep‑dives
- Identification of critical periods impacting results (e.g. April)

---

## ✅ Key Findings (Summary)
- Overall performance in the **first half of the year is negative** vs both **PY and Budget**
- Slight improvement observed only in:
  - **Gross Profit Margin YTD**
  - **COGS Ratio YTD**
- Other profitability metrics deteriorated due to:
  - Revenue decline
  - Insufficient reduction in Variable Costs and OPEX
- Contribution Margin and EBITDA show significant pressure on operational profitability
